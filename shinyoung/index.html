Value Investor Since 1996

Since establishment in 1996, Shinyoung has stayed true and loyal to its management philosophy
founded on value investments. Our unwavering identity is exactly what fuels our specialization and
unparalleled insight into Korea’s value investment sphere.

Long - Term Investment

We believe the best way to achieve superior returns is to focus on company fundamentals
and take a long-term view of the markets. Our research-driven investment process aims
to identify the investment opportunities with the best underlying fundamentals.

Rigorous & Independent Inhouse Research

We believe investing in assets trading at prices below their intrinsic values allows us
to achieve consistent positive risk-adjusted returns throughout market cycles.
We take particular pride in the quality and depth of our research and believe
it is what set us apart from our peers.
